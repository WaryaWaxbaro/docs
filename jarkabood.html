Jar Kabood Game

The game is between X's (Invisible opponent) and O's (Human player). Points are collected by jumping over one's opponent. Jumping can be performed in any direction. When human player needs to jump, first select where to jump from and then where to jump to. Jumping is not possible if immediate squares are empty, but moving is possible. Have a nice try.
